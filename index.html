Our story so far:


Our current unlikely heroes have found themselves caught and imprisoned on their very first session. It take not take them very long to figure out a way to escape the prison and find the first pieces of gear. Their curious nature had them stumble upon a torture room in which a wicked necromancer was doing experiments on unwilling subjects. Coming out of the battle as victors they discovered several other items and the first steps in to professions. The torture room became an unlikely place for a rest as it is also happingly avoided by the occupants of this place. The adventures continued their exploration of their surroundings and had a small skirimish with some bandits around a small encampment. Some tents may or may not have 'accidentally' caught on fire, but the enemies were disposed off quickly. Not resting on their laurels the players found a side cave with multiple slaves prisoners with jobs whom they set free. In this cave they found a strange device which seems to mind-control nearby entities. They turned off the device which set free a bugbear. The bugbear only spoke broken common, but was released by the group after a short conversation. This decision paid off quickly, when the group headed out again. At the entrance of what was now revealed to be a cave several corpses lay around with bugbear wounds. Exploring outside several more corpses where found, including the poor bugbear. Crawling outside at night made it possible to sneak around and the group found another cave. Exploring this cave they got ambushed and captured by what later turned out to be a group of rebels hiding from the pillaging of Trepa. After a short respite, our heroic adventurers once again travelled in to the darkness. Scouting around a bridge was found. When they tried crossing it their cover was blown and a small fight broke out. A small amount of luck and because of the bugbear his earlier rampage they werent discovered and managed to move on. Another cave was explored and it turned out to house somekind of temple. This is where the story paused.
